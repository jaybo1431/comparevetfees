## 💰 Monetization Strategy (3 Revenue Streams)

### **Stream 1: Lead Generation (Primary) — Launches Day 1**

**How It Works:**
1. User searches "vet prices Bournemouth"
2. Lands on our site, compares prices
3. Clicks "Get a Quote" on cheapest practice
4. Fills form (name, email, phone, pet type, service)
5. We email lead to practice
6. Practice contacts user
7. We charge practice £5-£15 per lead

**Pricing Tiers:**
- **Standard Enquiry:** £5 (general question)
- **Consultation Booking:** £10 (specific appointment)
- **Procedure Booking:** £15 (neutering, dental, etc.)

**Revenue Projections:**
- Month 1-3 (Dorset validation): 10 leads/week = £400/month
- Month 4-6 (Dorset traction): 50 leads/week = £2,000/month
- Month 7-12 (Regional expansion): 200 leads/week = £8,000/month

**Why Practices Pay:**
- Performance-based (no risk)
- Cheaper than Google Ads
- Qualified leads (pet type + service already known)
- Easy to track ROI

**Implementation:** 15 mins (just need Resend API key)

---

### **Stream 2: Featured Listings (Premium) — Month 4-6**

**How It Works:**
- Practices pay £99-£199/month for:
  - Top placement in search results
  - "Featured Practice" badge
  - Enhanced profile (photos, videos, special offers)
  - Priority in "Get a Quote" forms
  - Analytics dashboard (leads per month, conversion rates)

**Why Practices Pay:**
- Increased visibility = more leads
- Control their brand presentation
- See exact ROI (leads generated vs. cost)

**Revenue Projections:**
- 5 featured practices × £149/month = £745/month (Month 6)
- 10 featured practices × £149/month = £1,490/month (Month 12)

**Moat:** Only works if we have traffic. Once we have traffic, this is passive recurring revenue.

---

### **Stream 3: Practice Profiles (Subscription) — Month 6-12**

**How It Works:**
- Practices pay £49-£99/month to:
  - Claim their profile
  - Update their own prices
  - Respond to reviews
  - Post updates (new vets, equipment, hours)
  - Add special offers

**Why Practices Pay:**
- Control their own data (vs. us updating it)
- Build their brand
- Respond to customer feedback
- Keep prices current

**Revenue Projections:**
- 10 profiles × £69/month = £690/month (Month 9)
- 25 profiles × £69/month = £1,725/month (Month 12)

**Moat:** Requires critical mass. Once practices see competitors claiming profiles, they'll want theirs too.
