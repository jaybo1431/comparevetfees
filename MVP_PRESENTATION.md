## ✅ MVP Features Delivered

### **1. Dorset-First Coverage**
- **38 veterinary practices** across 10 Dorset towns
- Complete coverage of Bournemouth, Poole, Christchurch, Dorchester, Weymouth, Wimborne, Ferndown, Verwood, Blandford Forum, and Swanage
- Each practice includes:
  - Full address and contact details
  - Transparent pricing for 11 common procedures
  - Rating and review count
  - Independent vs corporate ownership (CVS, IVC Evidensia, Medivet, etc.)
  - Practice features (24hr Emergency, Cat-Friendly, etc.)
  - Year established

### **2. Transparency Scoring System**
Every practice has a **1-5 transparency score** displayed prominently — giving users confidence about which practices are most open about their pricing. This is a unique differentiator vs competitors.

### **3. Price Comparison Engine**
- Side-by-side pricing for 11 procedures:
  - Standard Consultation
  - Vaccination Course
  - Cat Neutering
  - Dog Neutering/Spaying
  - Dental Check & Clean
  - Microchipping
  - Written Prescription
  - Emergency Consultation
  - X-Ray & Blood Tests
- Instant benchmarking: see if each price is above/below/near the local average
- Full breakdown showing £ savings potential

### **4. Lead Generation System** ✨
**This is where the business model kicks in (£5-£15 per enquiry):**

- **"Get a Quote" buttons** on every practice page
- Clean, professional contact form that captures:
  - Customer name, email, phone
  - Pet type (Dog, Cat, Rabbit, Other)
  - Service needed (Consultation, Vaccination, Neutering, etc.)
  - Additional message
- Form submissions trigger notifications (ready for email integration)
- Direct contact info shown as backup (phone, website)
- "No booking fees" messaging builds trust

**Customer experience:**
1. Browse prices → find best practice
2. Click "Get a Quote"
3. Fill in quick form
4. Practice receives lead and contacts them directly
5. You earn £5-£15 per successful enquiry

### **5. SEO-Optimized Landing Pages**
11 location-specific pages for local search visibility:
- `/vets/bournemouth` — Bournemouth Vet Prices
- `/vets/poole` — Poole Vet Prices
- `/vets/christchurch` — Christchurch Vet Prices
- `/vets/dorchester` — Dorchester Vet Prices
- `/vets/weymouth` — Weymouth Vet Prices
- `/vets/wimborne`, `/vets/ferndown`, `/vets/verwood`, `/vets/blandford-forum`, `/vets/swanage`
- `/vets/dorset` — All Dorset practices overview

Each page includes:
- Local stats (avg prices, practice count)
- Price ranges for that town
- Links to nearby towns
- SEO-optimized titles and descriptions

**Goal:** Rank for "vet prices [town]" searches across Dorset

### **6. Core Pages**
- **Homepage** — Hero, search, practice cards, stats, CMA banner
- **About** — Mission, problem statement, Dorset-first strategy
- **How It Works** — 3-step explainer, data sourcing transparency
- **Practice Detail Pages** — Full price list, benchmarking, lead gen CTA

### **7. Professional Design**
- Clean, modern UI using emerald green brand color
- Fully responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Fast loading (statically generated pages)
- Trust signals throughout (CMA compliance, independence badges, transparency scores)
